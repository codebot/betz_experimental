Board: BETZ MINI, rev. A

PCB fabrication notes
====
 * 4 layers
 * green soldermask, white silkscreen
 * circular outline, 40mm diameter
 * 1.6mm finished thickness
 * minimum via drill 8mil
 * minimum trace/space 6mil
 * via-in-pad required on U4 (unusual footprint on power IC)

Assembly notes:
====
 * mostly single-sided SMT
 * four SMT capacitors on bottom side
 * no through-hole parts required for installation; we will do this ourselves
 * please ignore all parts with "Do Not Populate" (DNP) part numbers
